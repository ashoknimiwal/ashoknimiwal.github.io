About Me

I am Ashok Kumar, a PhD candidate at the Telfer School of Management, University of Ottawa, specializing in Digital Transformation and Innovation. I received my B.Tech (Hons.) in Manufacturing Science and Engineering and my M.Tech in Industrial Engineering and Management, both in 2022. My current research interests explore the intersection of finance and natural language processing, where I leverage neural networks to analyze large-scale textual data.
